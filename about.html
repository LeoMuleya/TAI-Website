Overview:

Founded in 2020, Thrive Africa Initiative Organisation (TAI) is a non-profit established in February 2022 to empower young people in art, tech, and media. TAI's mission is to offer a platform for youth to excel creatively and socially through mentorship, workshops, and community projects, aiming to foster a socially conscious generation. With a vision of enabling young creators to make impactful contributions, TAI's goal is to provide opportunities for marginalized youth to participate in socio-economic decisions. The organization upholds values such as transparency, creativity, and integrity. TAI's objectives include promoting cultural expression, raising awareness on social issues, supporting marginalized communities, and encouraging civic participation through art and media. Their activities encompass exhibitions, festivals, training, and collaborative projects to achieve their multifaceted objectives.span>

Mission:

TAI is committed to offering young people opportunities to explore, express, and excel in art, tech, and media. Through mentorship, workshops, and community projects, TAI nurtures creativity, encourages innovation, and fosters a generation of socially conscious creators.

Vision:

TAI envisions empowering the next generation of young artists, tech innovators, and media creators to unleash their potential and make a positive impact on their communities and the broader society.

Our Goals:

The goal of the Thrive Africa Initiative Organisation (TAI)
is to contribute to a unified and healthy world by providing children and youth, especially those marginalized, with the opportunity to participate in socio-economic development decisions that affect them.This involves creating platforms and opportunities for these groups to be actively involved and have their voices heard in community and societal development.
